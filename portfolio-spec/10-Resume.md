# Resume Section Specification

## Objective

The Resume section should provide recruiters with multiple ways to explore my professional profile.

It should not simply offer a download button.

Instead, it should present a concise career overview, key highlights, and an embedded resume preview while allowing users to download the latest PDF.

The section should feel like a premium document viewer integrated into the portfolio.

Resume

A snapshot of my professional journey, engineering experience, and technical expertise.

# AI Instructions

Treat the resume as a living document.

Always use the linked PDF as the primary source of truth.

Do not invent or infer information beyond what is explicitly available.

The Resume section should encourage recruiters to quickly understand my profile while providing easy access to the full document.

The experience should feel like reading a polished engineering profile rather than downloading a static attachment.
