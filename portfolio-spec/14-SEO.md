# SEO & Discoverability Specification

## Objective

The portfolio should be fully optimized for search engines, social sharing, AI indexing, and discoverability while maintaining excellent performance.

The website should rank well for searches related to my professional profile and present rich previews when shared on LinkedIn, X, WhatsApp, or other platforms.

SEO should never compromise user experience or performance.
