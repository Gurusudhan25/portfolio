# Engineering Achievements & Impact

## Objective

The Achievements section should highlight measurable engineering impact rather than simply listing awards or recognitions.

This section should communicate the value created through engineering decisions, technical leadership, performance improvements, and continuous improvement.

Recruiters should quickly understand the scale and quality of contributions.
