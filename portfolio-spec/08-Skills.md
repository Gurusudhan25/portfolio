# Technical Skills & Engineering Capabilities

## Objective

The Skills section should demonstrate technical depth through real-world engineering experience rather than subjective proficiency ratings.

Avoid visual elements that imply arbitrary skill levels such as:

- Percentage bars
- Circular progress indicators
- Star ratings
- Numeric scores

Instead, showcase technologies by explaining where and how they have been applied in production systems.

The goal is to communicate credibility through experience.

# AI Instructions

Do not estimate skill levels.

Do not display percentages.

Do not rank technologies arbitrarily.

Demonstrate expertise through production usage, engineering practices, and project references.

The Skills section should reinforce credibility by connecting every technology to real engineering work and measurable outcomes.
