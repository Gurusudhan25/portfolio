# Experience Section Specification

## Objective

The Experience section should showcase my professional growth as a Software Engineer.

This is NOT a resume timeline.

This is an Engineering Journey.

Each company should tell a story.

Every experience should explain:

- What the company builds
- What business problems were being solved
- My responsibilities
- My technical contributions
- Challenges I solved
- Technologies I worked with
- Engineering impact
- Business impact
- Lessons learned

The goal is to demonstrate ownership, engineering maturity, and real-world experience.

Professional Journey

Building products, solving business problems, and continuously improving software through thoughtful engineering.
